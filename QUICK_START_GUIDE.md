## How to Process a Sale

### Step-by-Step POS Guide

1. **Click "Point of Sale"** in the sidebar

2. **Add items to cart:**
   - Click "Add to Cart" on menu items
   - Use **+** and **-** buttons to change quantity
   - Click **Ã—** to remove an item

3. **Select payment method:**
   - Choose "Cash" or "GCash"
   - If Cash: Enter amount received
   - System shows change automatically

4. **Review cart** - Check items and quantities

5. **Click "Process Sale"** - Done!

> ğŸ’¡ **Tip**: For Cash payments, make sure customer gives you enough before clicking "Process Sale"!

### Common Scenarios

**Multiple items:**
- Add each item one by one OR
- Add once and change quantity number

**Customer changes mind:**
- Click Ã— button to remove item
- Or use - button to decrease quantity

**Large bills:**
- Enter exact amount in "Cash Received"
- Give customer the change shown

## Common Admin Tasks

### 1. Create New Staff

1. Go to **Manage Staff**
2. Fill in the form at top:
   - Full name
   - Email (unique)
   - Contact number
   - Password
   - Select stall
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. Staff receives confirmation email

### 2. Edit Staff

1. Go to **Manage Staff**
2. Click on any staff member
3. Edit details in popup
4. Click **"Update"**

> ğŸ’¡ **Note**: Email addresses cannot be changed

1. Go to **Manage Inventory**

4. Enter new price
5. Save

> âœ… Price changes apply immediately to all stalls

## Quick Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In?

- Check Caps Lock is off
- Verify email and password
- First time? Check email for confirmation link
- Still stuck? Contact admin

### POS Not Working?

- Refresh page (F5)
- Check internet connection
- Make sure payment method is selected
- Try logging out and back in

### Reports Show Nothing?

- Check date filters
- Staff: Make sure viewing "Today"
- Verify stall filter is correct
- Refresh page

### General Issues?

- Refresh your browser
- Clear cache and cookies
- Try different browser
- Contact IT support
